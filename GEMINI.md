# Directory Overview

This directory contains the materials for the "AI & Business Presentation" delivered by Gabriel Lacroix of Kaizen Strategic AI on October 23, 2025. The presentation was aimed at the Northern BC business community and focused on practical applications of AI for small and medium-sized enterprises.

The contents include the presentation itself, research materials, and a detailed prompt for a live coding demonstration that was part of the event.

# Key Files

*   **`AI-Business-Presentation-October-2025.pdf`**: The main presentation file.
*   **`README.md`**: Provides a comprehensive overview of the event, the presentation's content, and the structure of the repository.
*   **`LIVE_CODING_PROMPT.md`**: Contains the exact, detailed prompt given to an AI assistant (Cursor) to build a Next.js restaurant tip-out calculator application live during the presentation. This demonstrates the power of AI-assisted development.
*   **`RESEARCH_AND_CITATIONS.md`**: A document that aggregates the research, data sources, and citations that support the statistics and claims made in the presentation.
*   **`research/`**: This directory contains the raw research notes that informed the presentation's content.
    *   `AI-Business-Presentation-Insights.md`: A summary of market research data and strategic AI concepts.
    *   `Billion-Dollar-Marketing-Campaign-Theft-Research.md`: An analysis of marketing strategies from major corporations, adapted for small businesses.
    *   `Virtual-Board-Marketing-Research-Archive.md`: Insights from a "virtual board of directors" comprised of well-known business leaders, used to generate strategic marketing advice.

# Usage

The files in this directory are intended to be used as a reference for the content and research behind the AI & Business Presentation. The `LIVE_CODING_PROMPT.md` is a particularly interesting artifact, as it serves as a concrete example of how to prompt an AI for a complex software development task.
